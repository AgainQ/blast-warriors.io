Frequently Asked Questions

What is Blast Warriors?

BlastWarriors is an easy-to-use dApp where you can mint Bull or Bear themed NFTs, inspired by financial markets. It operates on the Blast L2 Network, ensuring that minting transactions are fast, cheap, and secure.

Is it safe to mint?

Yes, minting is safe. Our contracts have undergone thorough audits to ensure security. The only transactions you'll sign are for the safe minting of NFTs, and you will not give any permissions for transferring your funds. You can find the smart contract addresses in our documentation.

Can I mint multiple NFTs?

Absolutely! You you are welcome to mint as many NFTs as you desire. There is no hard cap on the number of nfts and individual can mint.

Is there any incetive to mint?

Yes indeed, users are rewarded with Blast Gold. All Gold that BlastWarriors receive from Blast will be distributed to our users based on the amount of NFTs they mint and frequency of interactions with our dApp.
